Science

Science is all about discovering the world around us. We learn how things work, why they happen, and explore different wonders of nature.





Animals

Animals are living creatures that can move, grow, and need food. Some animals live on land, some in water, and some can fly in the air.

Examples:

Dog, Fish, Bird, Lion





Plants

Plants are living things that grow in the ground. They need sunlight, water, and soil to grow. Trees, flowers, and vegetables are all types of plants.





The Weather

The weather tells us what it is like outside. It can be sunny, rainy, windy, or snowy. Weather changes every day and helps us decide what clothes to wear.

Examples:

Sunny, Rainy, Cloudy, Snowy





Space

Space is the huge area outside our planet where the stars, the moon, and other planets are. It is very big, and astronauts travel there to explore.

Example: The Moon, Mars, Stars
