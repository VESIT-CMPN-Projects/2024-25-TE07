About Us

We are best known as advocates for children with dyslexia around the globe. But our programs are not only for dyslexia. They are widely used for whole class phonics, maths, typing, and early reading instruction. We collaborate with some of the world's leading academics and follow the Science of Reading to ensure our programs are based upon proven research.

Our goal is to provide effective learning to all children at an affordable cost.

About Us

BrightMinds was born out of a deep desire to make a difference in the lives of those affected by dyslexia. Our founders, a group of educators, parents, and individuals with dyslexia, created a space where dyslexics and their supporters can find the help they need to succeed.

Why Dyslexia?

Dyslexia is one of the most common learning differences, yet it is often misunderstood. We aim to change that narrative by educating the public, promoting early intervention, and providing a platform for dyslexics to share their unique perspectives. We believe that with the right support, everyone with dyslexia can unlock their full potential.

Join Us

At BrightMinds, we believe in the power of support and connection. Whether you’re here to learn, share, or connect, we welcome you with open arms. Together, we can build a brighter future for people with dyslexia.
