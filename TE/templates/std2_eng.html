English for Class 2

Nouns

A noun is a naming word. It names a person, place, animal, or thing. Let's see some examples:

  1. Person: Teacher, Doctor, John 👩‍🏫👨‍⚕️
  2. Place: School, Park, City 🏫🏙️
  3. Animal: Dog, Lion, Elephant 🐶🦁
  4. Thing: Book, Chair, Ball 📚🪑

Verbs

A verb is an action word. It tells us what someone does. Examples of verbs:

  1. Jump: The boy jumps high. 🏃‍♂️
  2. Read: She reads a book. 📖
  3. Sing: They sing a song. 🎤

Sentence Formation

Sentences tell us something. A sentence starts with a capital letter and ends with a full stop (.). Let's practice:

  1. Example 1: The cat sleeps on the mat. 🐱
  2. Example 2: John plays with his dog. 🐶
  3. Example 3: I like to eat apples. 🍏
