Maths

Mathematics is the study of numbers, shapes, and patterns. It is an essential skill that helps us understand the world around us and solve everyday problems.





Addition

Addition is the process of calculating the total of two or more numbers. It is one of the four basic operations in arithmetic.

Example:

2 + 3 = 5





Subtraction

Subtraction is the process of finding the difference between two numbers. It helps us understand how much is left when one quantity is taken away from another.

A fraction is a way of representing a part of a whole. It is written as a ratio of two numbers, with the top number called the numerator and the bottom number called the denominator.
